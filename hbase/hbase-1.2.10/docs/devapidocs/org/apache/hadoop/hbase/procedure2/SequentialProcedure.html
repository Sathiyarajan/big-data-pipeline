    @InterfaceAudience.Private
@InterfaceStability.Evolving
public abstract class SequentialProcedure<TEnvironment>
extends Procedure<TEnvironment>
    A SequentialProcedure describes one step in a procedure chain. -> Step 1 -> Step 2 -> Step 3 The main difference from a base Procedure is that the execute() of a SequentialProcedure will be called only once, there will be no second execute() call once the child are finished. which means once the child of a SequentialProcedure are completed the SequentialProcedure is completed too.
